E-invoicing project

A business-facing page focused on digital invoicing and usability.

The project aims to reduce friction for small businesses that need to adapt to e-invoicing. The goal is not to add bureaucracy, but to provide a clear flow: create, convert, validate and transmit.

The problem

Many craftsmen still work with simple documents and limited tooling. The transition to e-invoicing may feel painful if the product is too heavy.

The promise

A clear interface that guides users from invoice creation to a structured and transferable format, with a practical rather than administrative mindset.

The positioning

A product focused on clarity, compatibility and support for real-life workflows used by small businesses and craftsmen.

The user journey

The user prepares an invoice through a simple interface or from an existing document.
The data is structured, checked and enriched where needed.
The document is converted into the required e-invoicing format.
The user follows the transmission status and keeps a clear audit trail.

The key features of this tool

  • Usability for non-technical users.
  • Production of documents in compliance with the law.
  • Compatible with super_PDP. The Approved Platform (PDP), that does not sell your data..
  • Personalised support throughout every stage of the legislative process.
  • Keeping the workflow simple despite regulatory complexity.
